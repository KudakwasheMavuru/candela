About Us.

Unsustainable firewood and charcoal use is the single largest source of greenhouse gas emissions (GHGs) in many developing countries like Rwanda and significantly exacerbates the negative effects of global climate change.Burning firewood and charcoal is closely linked to both forest degradation as well as to deforestation while increasing a region’s exposure to a host of other environmental risks such as soil loss, desertification, loss of biodiversity, and water scarcity.

To join the fight against climate change and advocate for "zero firewoood" in Rwanda, we created Candela Tech Ltd. It is a private company owned by Rwandan Nationals who introduced canvas biogas system in Rwanda in the year of 2013 and worked with the Rwanda Energy Group and districts to disseminate more than 6000 systems.

We do this by manufacturing and directly distributing smart cook-stoves generating electricity and biomass pelletizing mainly using rice husks. The Rwandan economy is well-aligned to support a parabolic uptake of smart cooking stoves & extensive range of biofuel pellet solutions in the next generation, thus, making the former three-stone-fire heating system a cooking style of the past.

The introduction of Candela Model 1 - A new era of cooking.

Minimises Smoke Emissions

Our stove has one of the cleanest multi-fuel cooking functionality in the world. It will minimise smoke emissions to a negligible level to mitigate the negative health effects of Household Air Pollution.

Reduces fuel needs

Our smart cookstove can burn any type of solid biomass fuel (crop residue, animal waste, or small sticks). This displaces the need for energy-dense harvested wood or charcoal. It also burns fuel more efficiently than traditional open fire stoves and thus uses 50-85% less fuel.

Provides access to electricity

The stove also has a functionality of generating electricity, giving users who ordinarily don’t have electricity access the facility to charge their phones or plug in the LED lamp attachment.

Accessible via flexible micro-loan

The stove will be sold on a 6-12 month micro-loan. Customers will use the energy savings achieved with the product to pay back the investment.This gives people living on $1 a day access to a RWF 100,000 product.
